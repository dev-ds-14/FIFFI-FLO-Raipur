Module 02
The FIRE Framework
ProprietaryLive Demo

Four moves to write prompts that actually work. Use FIRE every time you open Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini. The output changes the moment your prompt does.

F
Frame the role
Tell AI who it should become. "You are a nutritionist with 20 years of experience."
I
Instruct the task
Say what you want done in one clean sentence. Verb first. No fluff.
R
Reference the context
Give it details — audience, goal, constraints, and what not to do.
E
Express the format
How should the answer look? Table, bullets, email, script, 200 words.
